Guiding principles (the vision)

  • Separate human work from AI work — Plane (human) vs AppFlowy (agent) so agents can't corrupt curated schemas.
  • PHI never crosses untreated — air-gap DMZ de-identifies/anonymizes before data enters AMDG; neither side knows the other.
  • Consolidate & simplify; proven over custom — ~80% is off-the-shelf; spend rigor on the 20% showstoppers.
  • Data governance & sovereignty — keep control, but don't become a security company; outsource what isn't worth owning.

Open decisions (the amber)

  • Data warehouse host — Hetzner vs Azure vs AWS (Snowflake too costly). Cissy researching.
  • All-cloud vs hybrid-local inference — local box mirrored to cloud for redundancy.
  • Azure vs AWS vs mixed — Azure BAA out-of-box; AWS cheaper/BYO-model processing.
  • JumpCloud go/no-go — Mike: business case to Brian in 48h.
  • DevSecOps/SRE hire — HIPAA audit + reliability gap.
  • Config lenses — AMDG vs AIBs vs AMDG-for-AIBs.
